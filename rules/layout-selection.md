# Layout Selection

| Intent | Layout | Required fields |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Introduce the deck and its central promise | `cover` | eyebrow, title, subtitle |
| State one idea and bind it to supporting evidence | `key-message` | eyebrow, title, subtitle, visual, evidence |
| Contrast two operating models or states | `comparison` | title, left, right |

Do not use a comparison layout for unrelated lists. Do not use a key-message layout when the slide has more than one conclusion.
